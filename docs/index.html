The Personal Health Record System

The LHNCBC Personal Health Record (PHR) is a web-based tool that allows consumers to keep track of their own health information as well as that of their children and elderly or other dependents.

User Documentation

Documentation for the user of the PHR system is available in three different forms:

  2. Online documentation, available here; and
  3. A document in Microsoft Word or PDF format available for download to your system.
