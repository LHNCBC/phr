10. Restore an Inactive PHR

Inactive PHRs can be restored if desired. Inactive PHRs are placed at the bottom of the PHR Home page under the Inactive PHRs I Manage section of the PHR Home page. Once a PHR is made inactive, it can either be restored or deleted permanently.

To restore an inactive PHR:

  1. On the PHR Home page, locate the Inactive PHRs I Manage section at the bottom of the page.
  2. Click the Show link at the right side of the section heading to display all inactive PHRs you manage.
  3. Click the Restore button next to the PHR you would like to be restored.
  4. The PHR will be restored and placed back in the list of PHRs in the Personal Health Records I Manage section of the PHR Home page.
